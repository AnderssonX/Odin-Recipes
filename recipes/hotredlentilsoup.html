Hot red lentil soup

Back

Description

A spicy soup that will warm your soul and comfort your heart.

Ingredients

  • Red lentils, 2 dl
  • Coconut cream, 2 dl
  • Water, 3 dl
  • Crushed tomatoes, 1 can (300-400g)
  • Aubergine, 1
  • Zucchini, 1
  • Garlic, 3 cloves
  • Red paprika, 1
  • Cabbage, 100g
  • Basil, thyme, parsil or other fresh herbs
  • Chili flakes or fresh chili.
  • Vegetable stock, 2 cubes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, black pepper

    • Steps

    1. Rinse lentils in cold water.
    2. Peel the aubergine, dice the garlic and quicky fry in a very hot pan, some burned black spots is a good sign! Put aside when done
    3. Peal and chop the garlic.
    4. Heat up a large pot with some olive oil, add the garlic and the lentils, stir and fry for a few minutes.
    5. Add the tomatoes, coconut cream, water, paprika and aubergine to the pot, stir an let it come to a slow boil
    6. Cut the zucchini to fine cubes, slice the cabbage finely
    7. Mix the soup until it's smooth.
    8. Add the zucchini, cabbage, vegetable stock, chili and fresh herbs to the pot and let it boil for a few more minutes
    9. Add salt and black peppar to taste.
    10. Servce with some fresh bread and possibly sour cream for a smoother soup.
